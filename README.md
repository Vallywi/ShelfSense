## 🚨 The Problem

Every day, **2,175 tons of food** are thrown away in Metro Manila alone —
while nearly **30% of Filipinos still go hungry**.

It doesn't start at the trash bin.
**It starts at home** — with food pushed to the back of the pantry,
expiration dates no one checked, and no idea what to cook
with what's already there.

---

## ✨ What ShelfSense Does

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| 📦 **Pantry at a glance** | See what you have, what's expiring, and what to use first — all in one view |
| 🔍 **Smart item adding** | Scan barcodes, enter details, or estimate expiry — even when labels are missing |
| 🍳 **Recipe suggestions** | Get recipes built around what you have, prioritizing near-expiry items |
| 🤖 **Built-in assistant** | Ask *"what can I cook tonight?"* and get answers based on your actual pantry |
